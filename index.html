    About

    The Word Journal Blog

    Founded by an unshakeable passion for the gospel of Jesus Christ, Word Journal is a diary of sermons, teachings and revelation knowledge faithfully drawn from the Word of God. The site is also commited to sharing messages by independent writers who share the same vision of teaching the gospel of Jesus Christ to the whole world.

    The content ranges from print to video messages and and song ministrations. Our sole purpose on earth is to worship God in spirit and in truth. We can only worship God right when we know Him. On this site, we publish the truth as it is written in God's word. God bless everyone.
