So what if your skin is darker than the rest?



Black Women are mostly curvy

A team of evolutionary scientists from New Zealand have suggested that the male attraction to a large female bottom is due to an ingrained preference for women with a small 'waist to hip ratio'. The smaller a woman's waist in comprison with her hips, the more attracive she tends to be in the perception of most men.

Dr. John Ong'ech of KNH stated that a certain amount of fat in a woman's body is necessary to boost her fertility. Baby's are naturally better protected in the womb of a sizable woman than in the womb of a very skinny one. Even though the voluptuousness of a woman can sometimes cause alot of unwanted attention and underlying problems for her, but curvy women who love their bodies are simply happy women. I most groups pf people around the world, the body size of a woman appears to be an obvious indicator of physical and psychological maturity.


Black women age gracefully

It is a popular saying that blacks don't crack. This is mostly realised when black women of the same age but from different races have their looks compared with, and mirrored against each other. It takes a lot more time for a black woman to age. Thanks to the rich supply of malanin in her skin. Darker skin tends to age more slowly than lighter skin because it has more melanin. The human skin gets damaged due lots of exposure to the sun. The more melanin that is present in the skin, the more protection it gives the skin from the inevitable photoaging process. So chew on this saying every black woman..."The Methuselah, or “ageless,” gene was found twice as often in black women as in Caucasian women." and feel good about it


Black women wear the most controversial hairstyles

All people from all races in the world apart from the african race seem to have all kinds of convenient and easy to manage hair types. Their hair textures range from straight to wavy or curly strands. But black race in their predicament, they painfully have their hair type falling between varying degrees of tightly curled strands. The curls could spiral, coil loop or zig zag. All these attributes make it very difficult for the black person to grow and maintain their hair painlessly. The kinky black hair would rather grow staying up than grow falling down. The black hair defies gravity. So if the black woman has to wear a good looking hair then she has to spend a lot of money and time to achieve this good look. Black hair is a big deal, and for the stronng black woman, her hair-do is a statement she makes. If she has to make her hair, then she has to make a bold statement with it.
